   #+end_src

** Bind C-c a to =org-store-agenda=
   #+begin_src emacs-lisp
     (global-set-key (kbd "C-c a") 'org-agenda)
   #+end_src

** Bind C-c c to =org-capture=
   #+begin_src emacs-lisp
     (global-set-key (kbd "C-c c") 'org-capture)
   #+end_src
